GRAPHIC DESIGN

I am a very visual person and I find a lot of my ideas can be transferred using graphic design. My work tranlates the passion and effort I display when creating these pieces. Forming an identity through this art form has been a challenging, yet enjoyable journey. I've payed a lot of attention on what makes designs aesthetically pleasing to an audience as well as for myself. I always strive to create good quality work.

My Work
