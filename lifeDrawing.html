LIFE DRAWING

Life drawing is one of my favourite activites. I feel much more connected with humanity, as I am constantly studying a person's gestures and form. It is highly theraputic and it has helped me with things like, having a focused mindest, empathy building and my mental well-being. Life drawing also made me realise that it allows you to notice details that can be easily overlooked, such as slight changes in posture, meaning that you have to quickly adapt to changes as well as having good patience.
