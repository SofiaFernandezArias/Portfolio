LIFE DRAWING

Throughout my process of building my portfolio, I've understood the beauty of life drawing like never before and I've yet so much to learn from it. Unfortunately, my school isn't providing session until after my portfolio deadline, so I've tried my best to find different ways on how to understand human anatomy on my own. However, there are various life sessions that I will be attending in early January.
