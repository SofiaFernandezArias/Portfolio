About Me

I am a person who loves to grasp onto any opportunity that can guide me into growing as an artist. Computer animation and VFX is where my greatest interest lies. I have many transferrable skills that I know will allow me to adapt quickly in this feild, such as confident knowledge and experience with Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Lightroom, Blender, and additionally, my drawing skills. Why animation? Well, I love art and technology, and there's nothing better than animation perfectly combining the two together, in order to create limitless visual imaginations. It is my dream to immerse myself in this industry, and I am full of potential, keen on learning anything new.
